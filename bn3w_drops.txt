Dropped reward depends on Busting LV. and MegaMan's remaining HP compared to his max HP including NaviCust boosts.
 
Busting LV. S+ and S++ can be achieved in Custom Style or Team Style under specific conditions. They will show as LV. S on the results screen, but internally the level is higher. Note that all NetNavis drop the same chips at LV. S+ and LV. S++.
 
Custom Style LV. S+:
- VS viruses.
- Achieved Busting LV. S.
- Delete Time < 5 seconds.
- Did not fire regular Buster shots or non-elemental Charge Shots (Heat/Aqua/Elec/Wood Style Power Attack is fine).
- Did not select any Navi chips on the Custom Screen, even if you don't use them (non-Navi screen-dimming chips are fine).
- Re-entered Custom Screen at least once (use FullCust or FastGauge).
 
Custom Style LV. S++:
- VS viruses.
- Achieved Busting LV. S.
- Delete Time < 5 seconds.
- Did not fire regular Buster shots or non-elemental Charge Shots (Heat/Aqua/Elec/Wood Power Attack is fine).
- Did not select any Navi chips on the Custom Screen, even if you don't use them (non-Navi screen-dimming chips are fine).
- Did not re-enter Custom Screen.
 
Team Style LV. S+:
- VS Navis.
- Achieved Busting LV. S.
- Delete time < 15 seconds.
 
Team Style LV. S++:
- VS Navis.
- Achieved Busting LV. S.
- Delete time < 20 seconds.
